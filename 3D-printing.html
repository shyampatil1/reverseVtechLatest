Types of 3D Printing
FDM(Fused Disposition Modeling)
Fused Disposition Modelling is a layer by layer used filament to creat the 3D objects.
* Accuracy = ± 0.80mm to 1mm
SLA(Sterio Litheo Graphy)
SLA is a form of 3D Printing technology used for creating 3D model(prototype) by using photochemical process by which light causes chemical monomarse to cross link together to form polymers.
* Accuracy = ± 0.50mm to 0.8mm
SLS(Selective Laser Sintering)
SLS 3D is used for both prototyping of functional polymer components and for small production runs as it offers a very high design freedom, high accuracy.
* Accuracy = ± 0.05mm to 0.3mm
